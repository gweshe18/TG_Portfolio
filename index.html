POTPREV Study Findings

In the POTPREV study, I conducted an in-depth analysis of predictors for nasal carriage, urinary tract infections, and bloodstream infections in hospitals across South Africa. Utilizing advanced statistical modeling in R Studio and data wrangling in Python, I uncovered associations between demographic factors such as age, gender, and HIV status with infection rates.
